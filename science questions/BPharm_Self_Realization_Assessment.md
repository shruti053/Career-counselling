# B.Pharm Self-Realization Assessment
## For Students of Classes 10–12 Considering Pharmacy

**Purpose:** This assessment helps you discover how you naturally observe, question, and connect scientific ideas with practical outcomes. It is designed to create self-awareness about whether pharmaceutical thinking aligns with your natural style. There are no "right" or "wrong" answers in the traditional sense — every choice reveals something meaningful about how your mind works.

**Instructions:** Read each question carefully. Choose the option that feels most natural to you. Take your time. Reflect on why you chose what you did.

**Total Questions: 40**

## SECTION A — Chemistry in Everyday Life (Questions 1-6)

**Category:** Chemistry in Everyday Life

After completing all 40 questions:

1. Review your chosen options across sections.

**Remember:** Pharmacy combines deep scientific understanding with practical application, precision, and responsibility. It rewards those who naturally wonder how things work, notice how small details matter, and enjoy turning scientific knowledge into useful, safe solutions.
